		Difference between SDLC and STLC

SDLC:-

SDLC stands for Software Development life cycle.

SDLC is a sequence of different activities performed during the software development process.

it is  mainly related to software development.

It helps in developing good quality software.
 
SDLC involves total six phases or steps.

In SDLC, more number of developers are required for the whole process.

In SDLC, development team makes the plans and designs.

Goal of SDLC is to complete successful development of software.


		
STLC:-

STLC stands for Software Testing Life Cycle.

STLC is a sequence of different activities performed during the software testing process.

It helps in making the software defects free.

STLC involves only five phases or steps.

It focuses only on testing the software.

In SDLC, more number of developers are required for the whole process.

In STLC, testing team makes the plans and designs.

Goal of STLC is to complete successful testing of software.
